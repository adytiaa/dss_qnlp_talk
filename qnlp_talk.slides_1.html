Pennylane Library¶

  • Cross-platform Python library for programming quantum computers

  • Differentiable programming paradigm enables the execution and training of quantum programs on various backends (built-in automatic differentiation)

  • PennyLane connects quantum computing with powerful machine learning frameworks like NumPy’s autograd, JAX, PyTorch, and TensorFlow, making them quantum-aware

For hands on please follow the tutorials https://pennylane.ai/qml/demos/¶

Quantum Natural Language Processing: QNLP¶

  • Cambridge Quantum have leveraged mathematical principles to build a bridge between quantum theory and linguistic structures

  • Language is “quantum native”

  • Pioneering work by Bob Coecke and Aleks Kissinger

  • Sentences as networks, not just a “bag of words”, where words interact in a particular fashion
